Good Henry

Good henry is a good version of henry, however paradoxical that may be as Henry is already as good as can be.

Appearance

Good Henry appears to be a yellow humanoid blob. He has an white "h" on his face, and his eyes are pure white circles. Good Henry has large white angelic wings with two yellow halos lopsided on his head, and floating rings on his arms and legs.

Personality

Good henry is a kindhearted person and would do no wrong. He is very kind and love to be kind at any time He likes Sam Sparks from cloudy with a chance of meatballs. Oddly enough he is the first henry who likes an character from an movie.
