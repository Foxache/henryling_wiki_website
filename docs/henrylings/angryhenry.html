Angry Henry

One of the first Henrylings is known as Angry Henry.

Appearance

Angry Henry appears to be a humanoid red blob, with a black h letter on their face. He has short dark red hair with a fringe, Angry Henry, like every other henryling, has big circular pure white eyes and a lowercase h on their face. Distinct from other henry's, Angry Henry usually has a black h instead of the regular white.

Personality

Angry Henry is know for being angry all the time, and can take it a bit too far, like how he once set Yrneh on fire.
